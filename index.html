We aim to dispel the notion that the digital marketing world is incomprehensible and hard to navigate by providing you with definitions of metrics and buzzwords, ad calculators and examples of what these terms mean in the real world - all in one place.

Metrics

Digital Media Metrics is split into "Brand" or "Performance" metrics. Brand refers to metrics relating to branding objectives (e.g. Impressions and Reach) while Performance refers to metrics relating to influencing a user to take a specific action (e.g. Click or a Conversion).

Look out for the calculator icon - - these pages feature ad calculators that help you calculate a metric for you.
